Diagrammi DI Box
================

DI Box passiva
==============

- 1 input xlr/jack combo unbalance
- 1 output thru jack unbalance
- 1 output xlr balance
- 1 ground/lift switch

DI Box passiva pad
==============

- 1 input xlr/jack combo unbalance
- 1 output thru jack unbalance
- 1 output xlr balance
- 1 ground/lift switch
- 1 pad switch 4 postitions for output xlr: 0bd, -xdb, -xdb, -xdb
